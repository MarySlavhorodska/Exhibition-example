“We will fight as long as we live and when we are gone there will be others to fight”

This AK 74 assault rifle was taken by the Ukrainian 25th Siecheslav brigade after the deoccupation of the city of Izyum, where the Russians had been for more than six months.

This period of time for a town in the Kharkiv region was the most terrible since the Second World War: tortured and killed civilians, completely wiped from the face of the earth or mutilated buildings and a complete ban on everything Ukrainian - this is what the "Russian peace" brought with it to the once happy and prosperous city.

Exhumation of bodies in the mass grave near Izyum. It was 447 bodies were found, including women and children. Photo "Suspilne".

Izyum, at the time of deoccupation, was the most air-bombed city in Ukraine, the Russian side bombed it even when the Russian flag flew over the city.

Photo from the Facebook page of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine. Effects of the bombing of Izium.

However, thanks to our soldiers, on September 14, the Ukrainian flag was raised again over Izyum.
